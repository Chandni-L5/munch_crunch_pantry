Frequently Asked Questions

Have a question about Munch Crunch Pantry, our wholefoods, or how we deliver? You might find the answer below. If not, get in touch and we’ll be happy to help.

We aim to dispatch all orders within 1–2 working days. Once shipped, standard delivery usually takes 2–4 working days, and express delivery 1–2 working days, depending on your location and our courier.

For more details, see our Shipping & Delivery page.

We currently deliver to addresses within the United Kingdom. If you’re outside the UK and would like to order, please contact us and we’ll see if we can help.

Yes! We sometimes offer free standard delivery when your basket reaches a certain total. Any current free delivery threshold will be clearly shown at checkout and in our banners.

Check the Shipping & Delivery page or homepage banner for the latest offer.

If your order arrives damaged, faulty, or not as expected, please contact us within 3 days of delivery with your order number, photos (if possible), and a short description of the issue.

You can read more on our Returns & Refunds page.

Many of our products contain or are packed in an environment that handles nuts, peanuts, sesame, gluten, and soy. Because of this, we can’t guarantee that any product is completely free from traces of allergens.

Always check the packaging for the latest ingredient and allergen information. If you have a severe allergy, please contact us before ordering.

For best freshness, we recommend keeping your wholefoods in an airtight container, stored in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight.

Some products can also be kept in the fridge or freezer for extended freshness – always check the packaging or product page for any specific storage advice.

If you have a valid discount or promo code, you can enter it at checkout in the “Discount Code” field. Once applied, you’ll see the updated total before you complete payment.

Some codes may have expiry dates or minimum spend requirements – these will be mentioned wherever the code is shared (e.g. newsletter or homepage banner).

You can usually checkout as a guest, but creating an account makes future shopping much easier. With an account, you can:

  • Save delivery details for faster checkout
  • View your order history
  • Manage your details and preferences

You can create an account when you checkout, or via the Sign Up page.
