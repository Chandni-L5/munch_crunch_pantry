Privacy Statement

This Privacy Statement explains how Munch Crunch Pantry (“we”, “us”, “our”) collects, uses, stores, and shares your personal information when you use our website and services.

1. What information we collect

  • Account details: name, email address, password (stored securely in encrypted/hashed form).
  • Order details: delivery address, phone number, items purchased, order totals, and order history.
  • Payment information: payments are processed by our payment provider (e.g., Stripe). We do not store your full card details.
  • Website usage data: basic analytics such as pages visited and actions taken (where enabled).
  • Support messages: if you contact us, we keep a record of the conversation to help resolve your request.

2. How we use your information

  • To provide and manage your account.
  • To process orders, payments, delivery, and customer support.
  • To send essential service emails (e.g., order confirmations and delivery updates).
  • To improve our website, products, and customer experience.
  • To comply with legal obligations (e.g., accounting and tax requirements).

3. Legal bases for processing (UK GDPR)

We process personal data under the following legal bases:

  • Contract: to provide products/services you request (e.g., fulfilling an order).
  • Legitimate interests: to operate and improve our business (balanced against your rights).
  • Legal obligation: where we must comply with the law.
  • Consent: for optional communications like marketing (where applicable).

4. Sharing your information

We only share your data when necessary to run our service, including:

  • Payment processors (e.g., Stripe) to take payment securely.
  • Delivery/logistics providers to ship your order.
  • Technical service providers (e.g., hosting, storage) to keep our site running.

We do not sell your personal data.

5. Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies to enable core site functionality (such as keeping items in your basket) and, where enabled, to understand how visitors use our website. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

6. Data retention

We keep personal information only as long as needed for the purposes described above, including legal and accounting requirements. You can request deletion of your account where we have no legal reason to keep the data.

7. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Request deletion of your data (where applicable).
  • Object to or restrict certain processing.
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.
  • Data portability (in certain cases).

8. Security

We use appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect personal information. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, so we can’t guarantee absolute security.

9. International transfers

Some of our service providers may process data outside the UK. Where this occurs, we use appropriate safeguards in line with data protection laws.
